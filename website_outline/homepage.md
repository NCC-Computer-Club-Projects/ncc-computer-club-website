## Our Mission  

We, the students of Northampton Community College's computer and information technology sector, learn many skills through our studies, but we sometimes struggle to find practical applications for those skills outside the classroom. Our members possess the potential to create meaningful projects for themselves and the community using not only what they learn at Northampton Community College, but also the skills they develop through independent research and hard work. The Computer Club gives students the opportunity to put their knowledge into practice through competitions, projects, and community involvement. The club also helps guide students toward valuable resources, including certification preparation, portfolio development, exam preparation, interview practice, and other career-building opportunities.

Through vision and collaboration, the members of the Northampton Community College Computer Club are committed to helping one another on the journey toward computer wizardry.
