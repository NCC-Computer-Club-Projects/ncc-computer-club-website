# Activities Page Outline

Follow this general outline when designing the **Activities** page.

## Competitions

- Include a slideshow presentation of each of the club's established competition teams, as well as competitions we plan to attend in the future
- Each image should have accompanying text describing the hosting organization, the competencies being tested, and participating rules. In a separate section, include the price per person, the location of the next event, and who to contact to join the team
